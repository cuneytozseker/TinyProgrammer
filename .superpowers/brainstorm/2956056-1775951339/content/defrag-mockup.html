Animation Phases

  1. Scan — highlights blocks sequentially (left-to-right, top-to-bottom), briefly flashing each yellow
  2. Consolidate — fragmented (red) blocks animate to fill gaps, free space (white) gets pushed to the end
  3. Verify — quick pass turning blocks blue one by one with a slight delay
  4. Complete — progress bar fills, status shows "Drive C: is 0% fragmented", pause, then exit
